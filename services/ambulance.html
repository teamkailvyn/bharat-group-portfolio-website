Types of Ambulance Services

Different ambulance types for various medical emergencies

PTS - Patient Transport

Non-emergency patient transportation for routine medical appointments and transfers

  • Basic medical equipment
  • Comfortable stretcher
  • Trained attendant
  • Oxygen support
₹15 - ₹20/km
BLS - Basic Life Support

Basic medical care and transportation for stable patients with medical supervision

  • EMT trained staff
  • Basic life support equipment
  • Oxygen and suction
  • First aid supplies
₹25 - ₹35/km
ALS - Advanced Life Support

Advanced medical care with life-support equipment and paramedics for critical patients

  • Paramedic team
  • Advanced life support
  • Cardiac monitor
  • Ventilator support
₹40 - ₹60/km
AAS - Air Ambulance

Emergency air transportation for critical cases and remote locations with medical team
