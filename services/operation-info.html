Common Surgical Procedures

Information about frequently performed operations

Cardiac Surgery

Heart surgeries including bypass, valve replacement, and angioplasty

Duration: 3-6 hours
Recovery: 4-8 weeks
Cost Range: ₹2,00,000 - ₹8,00,000
Orthopedic Surgery

Joint replacement, fracture repair, and spine surgeries

Duration: 1-4 hours
Recovery: 6-12 weeks
Cost Range: ₹1,50,000 - ₹5,00,000
Eye Surgery

Cataract, LASIK, retinal surgeries, and other eye procedures

Duration: 30 min - 2 hours
Recovery: 1-4 weeks
Cost Range: ₹25,000 - ₹1,50,000
Gynecological Surgery

Hysterectomy, ovarian surgery, and other women's health procedures

Duration: 1-3 hours
Recovery: 2-6 weeks
Cost Range: ₹75,000 - ₹3,00,000
