Types of Ambulance Services

Emergency medical transportation for every situation

Patient Transport Service

Rever Health Solution's Patient Transport Service in Kolkata can meet all your non-emergency medical transportation needs. With extensive experience and proven expertise in patient transport services, our team of skilled drivers and basic medical assistants deliver seamless care to patients requiring routine medical appointments, working professionally for scheduled transfers.

Basic Life Support

The Basic Life Support is a large van with key basic medical facilities. Not equipped to sustain unstable patients for a prolonged duration. These are larger vehicles like Tempo or Force which can carry up to 4 people with patient, providing essential medical care during transport.

Basic First Aid
Medical Kit
Wheelchair Support

Basic Monitors

Advanced Life Support

A large vehicle, Advanced Life Support (ALS) is utilized for the transportation of critically ill patients. Equipped with a comprehensive array of Intensive Care Unit (ICU) equipment, the ambulance features an integrated ventilator, oxygen cylinder, blood pressure monitor, and specialized medications necessary for critical care. This state-of-the-art vehicle is designed to provide life-sustaining interventions during transport, ensuring the highest level of care for patients in crisis.

Air Ambulance Service

Emergency air transportation for critical cases and remote locations. Our air ambulance service provides rapid medical transport with full ICU capabilities, ensuring patients receive immediate care during flight. Equipped with advanced life support systems and staffed by specialized medical teams for the most critical situations.
